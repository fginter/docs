Degree: degree of comparison

Degree is relevant for adjectives and some adverbs in Hungarian.

Treebank Statistics (UD_Hungarian)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

2699 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 1308 types (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 1124 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: hu-pos/ADJ (2534; 10% instances), hu-pos/ADV (165; 1% instances).

DET:

### Definition Determiners are words that modify [nouns](NOUN) or noun phrases and express the reference of the noun phrase in context. SYM:

### Definition A symbol is a word-like entity that differs from ordinary words by form, function, or both. Many symbols are or contain special non-alphanumeric characters, similarly to [punctuation](PUNCT). What makes them different from punctuation is that they can be substituted by normal words. This involves all currency symbols, e.g. _$ 75_ is identical to _seventy-five dollars_. Mathematical operators form another group of symbols. Another group of symbols is emoticons and emoji.

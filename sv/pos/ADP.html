ADP: adposition

Definition

Adposition is a cover term for prepositions and postpositions.

Swedish has prepositions but not postpositions (except in a few fixed archaic phrases). In Swedish, some prepositions may also function as verb particles, as in säga till “tell” or komma åt “get at”. They are still tagged ADP and not PART.

Examples

  • i “in/on”
  • på “of/at”
  • genom “through/via”
